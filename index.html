Phase One

The Reproductive Years

Ages 20s–30s · Estrogen + Progesterone in flux

Your reproductive years are a time of energy, ambition, and powerful hormonal cycles — but they're also when many women first notice that something is off. Irregular periods, PMS, PCOS, fertility challenges, postpartum hormonal crashes, and stress-driven cortisol imbalances are all signs that your hormones need support. Most women are told this is "normal." It isn't — and it doesn't have to be your normal.

🌱

Signs Your Hormones Are Off Balance

  • Irregular, heavy, or painful periods that disrupt your life
  • Severe PMS — mood swings, bloating, cravings, headaches
  • PCOS, endometriosis, or fertility challenges
  • Unexplained weight gain despite healthy eating and exercise
  • Postpartum depression, hair loss, or prolonged recovery
  • Chronic stress, burnout, and cortisol dysregulation
  • Skin issues, acne, or hair thinning linked to androgens

  • Cortisol management through food, movement, and somatic practices
  • Gut and liver support for healthy estrogen clearance

02
Phase Two

The Perimenopausal Transition

Ages 35–50 · Estrogen fluctuating · Progesterone declining

Perimenopause can begin up to 10 years before your last period — and most doctors miss it entirely. The anxiety that came from nowhere, the weight gain despite nothing changing, the 3am wake-ups, the brain fog, the irritability — this is your hormonal transition beginning. It's not aging. It's biology. And with the right support, it can be one of the most transformative phases of your life.

🌕
The shifting cycle

Signs You're in Perimenopause

  • Weight gain especially around the abdomen — despite no change in diet
  • Sleep disruption — waking at 3am, light sleeping, exhaustion
  • Hot flashes and night sweats — even before periods stop
  • Anxiety that appeared suddenly with no clear cause
  • Brain fog, memory issues, and difficulty concentrating
  • Irregular cycles — shorter, longer, heavier, lighter, or absent
  • Mood shifts, irritability, or feeling like a different person

How Coaching Transforms This Phase

  • Cortisol-smart nutrition with high protein + carb cycling
  • Cycle syncing adapted for irregular and IUD cycles
  • Cortisol-first workout plan — builds muscle without making symptoms worse
  • Somatic exercises to discharge cortisol and calm the nervous system
  • HRT-compatible supplement and oil protocols
  • Liver and gut detox to support healthy hormone elimination
  • Reframing perimenopause as an ascent, not a decline

03
Phase Three

The Menopausal Renaissance

Ages 50s+ · Post-menopausal renewal and empowerment

Menopause is not an ending — it is a renaissance. When estrogen settles at its new baseline, women gain clarity, purpose, and a powerful sense of self that many describe as the best chapter of their lives. The challenge is navigating the transition symptoms and building the nutritional and lifestyle foundation that protects your bones, heart, brain, and metabolism for the decades ahead.

Rising into your power

Challenges Women Face In Menopause

  • Persistent hot flashes and night sweats affecting sleep quality
  • Accelerated bone density loss — osteoporosis risk increases sharply
  • Cardiovascular risk increases as estrogen's protective effects decline
  • Vaginal dryness, bladder changes, and intimacy challenges
  • Brain health concerns — memory, focus, and cognitive sharpness
  • Metabolic changes — muscle loss, insulin resistance, weight shifts
  • Loss of identity or purpose in this new chapter of life

How Coaching Supports Your Renaissance

  • Bone-protective strength training and impact exercise protocols
  • Heart-healthy nutrition: omega-3s, fiber, antioxidants, and magnesium
  • Brain health strategy: creatine, DHA, cognitive lifestyle practices
  • HRT-compatible supplement and essential oil protocol
  • Hot flash management: cooling roller, herbal teas, yoga nidra
  • Metabolic protection: muscle-first nutrition and insulin optimization
  • Identity and purpose coaching for this powerful new chapter
